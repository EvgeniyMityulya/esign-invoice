Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This app ("eSign & Invoice", "the App") is developed by Evgeniy Mityulya ("we"). This policy explains what happens to your data.

Your documents stay on your device

Documents, signatures, clients, invoices, and everything you add to them are stored only on your device. We do not have a server for your content. We never upload it, never receive it, and cannot access or view it. No account and no sign-up are required to use the App.

What our service providers process

The App uses a few standard services that handle limited technical data. None of them receive your documents.

  • Apple and Adapty handle purchases and subscriptions. This covers your purchase and subscription status and an app-generated identifier, used to unlock features, manage your subscription, and restore purchases.
  • TelemetryDeck provides privacy-first, anonymous usage analytics. It receives aggregated, non-identifying signals about which features are used, so we can improve the App. It collects no personal data and does not track you across other apps or websites.
  • Sentry provides crash and diagnostics reporting. If the App crashes it sends technical data such as the error, device model, and iOS version so we can fix it. It does not include your document content.

What we do not do

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites. We do not access your documents or signatures.

Data retention

Data you create stays on your device until you delete it or remove the App. Technical data held by the services above is retained under their own policies.

Children

The App is a business tool and is not directed to children.

Your choices

You control on-device data directly by deleting documents or the App. For any request about the technical data above, email us and we will help.

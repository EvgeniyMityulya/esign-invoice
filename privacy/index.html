Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 July 2026

eSign & Invoice ("the App") is developed by Evgeniy Mityulya ("we", "us"). Your privacy is the point of this app. Your documents are yours, they stay on your device, and we cannot see them. This policy explains exactly what the App accesses, why, and what leaves your phone.

1. The short version

  • No account and no sign-up.
  • Your documents, signatures, clients, invoices, and estimates are stored only on your device.
  • We have no server for your content. We never upload it, receive it, or read it.
  • We do not sell your data, show ads, or track you across other apps.

2. Device permissions the App asks for

The App only asks for a permission when you use the feature that needs it. You can decline, or change your mind later in the iPhone Settings app.

Camera

Used to scan a paper document and to capture a signature from paper. The camera image is processed on your device to build a clean PDF or a signature. It is not sent anywhere.

Photos

When you choose to import a document, an image, or a business logo, iOS lets you pick specific items from your photo library. Only the items you pick are brought into the App, and they stay on your device. The App does not scan or read your whole library.

Contacts

When you add a client, you can pick one contact through the standard iOS contact picker. Only the contact you select is imported (name, email, phone, address) so you do not have to retype it. The App does not read your full address book.

3. What is stored, and where

Everything you create in the App is stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. This includes documents and their pages, signatures, stamps and form fields you place, clients, invoices, estimates, your business profile, and your logo. None of it is uploaded to us or to anyone else.

4. Technical data handled by service providers

To run the App we use a small number of standard services. None of them receive your documents or signatures.

  • Apple and Adapty handle purchases and subscriptions. They process your purchase and subscription status and an app-generated identifier, used to unlock features, manage your subscription, and restore purchases. We never see your card details; payment is handled entirely by Apple.
  • TelemetryDeck provides privacy-first, anonymous usage analytics. It receives aggregated signals about which features are opened, so we can see what to improve. It does not collect personal data and does not identify you or track you across other apps or websites.
  • Sentry provides crash reporting. If the App crashes it sends technical diagnostics such as the error, device model, and iOS version so we can fix the bug. It does not include your document content.

5. What we do not do

  • We do not sell or rent your data.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • We do not track you across other companies' apps or websites.
  • We do not access, upload, or store your documents or signatures.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your content stays on your device until you delete a document or remove the App. Deleting the App removes its on-device data. Technical data held by the providers in section 4 is kept under their own retention policies.

7. Children

The App is a business tool for signing and invoicing. It is not directed to children.

8. Your rights and choices

You control your on-device data directly by deleting documents or the App, and you control each permission in the iPhone Settings app. For any question about the technical data in section 4, email us and we will help.

9. International use

The App is offered in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, and Australia. Because your content is processed on your device, it is handled under the laws of the country where you use your phone.

10. Third-party privacy policies

  • Adapty: adapty.io/privacy
  • TelemetryDeck: telemetrydeck.com/privacy
  • Sentry: sentry.io/privacy

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App changes. The date at the top shows the latest version, and material changes will be reflected here.

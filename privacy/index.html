Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 July 2026

This app ("eSign & Invoice", "the App") is developed by Evgeniy Mityulya ("we"). This policy explains what happens to your data.

Your documents stay on your device

Documents, signatures, and everything you add to them are processed and stored only on your device. We never upload them, never receive them, and cannot access or view them. No account is required to use the App.

What is processed by our service providers

To run the App we rely on a few trusted services that process limited technical data:

  • Purchases and subscriptions are handled by Apple and by Adapty. This includes your purchase and subscription status and an app-generated identifier, used to manage your subscription and restore purchases.
  • Anonymous usage analytics come from TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first service. It receives aggregated, non-identifying signals about how features are used, so we can improve the App. It does not collect personal data and does not track you across other apps or websites.
  • Crash and diagnostics come from Sentry. If the App crashes it sends diagnostic data such as the error, device model, and iOS version so we can fix it. It does not include your document content.

What we do not do

We do not sell your data, we do not use it for advertising, we do not track you across other companies' apps or websites, and we do not access your documents or signatures.

Data retention

Data you create stays on your device until you delete it or remove the App. Technical data held by the services above is retained according to their own policies.

Children

The App is a business tool and is not directed to children.

Your choices

You control on-device data directly by deleting documents or the App. For any request about the technical data above, email us and we will help.

Third-party policies

  • Adapty: adapty.io/privacy
  • TelemetryDeck: telemetrydeck.com/privacy
  • Sentry: sentry.io/privacy

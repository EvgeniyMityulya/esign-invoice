Frequently asked questions

How do I sign a document?

Open the app, add a document (import a PDF, scan a paper page, or pick a photo), tap the signature tool, draw your signature or snap it from paper, place it, and share.

Where are my documents stored?

Only on your device. We never upload your documents or signatures to any server and we cannot see them.

Is my signature legally valid?

A drawn or photographed signature placed with the app is a simple electronic signature and is recognised as valid for everyday agreements in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia. This is general information, not legal advice. Some documents, such as wills or property deeds, may require other formalities.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed by Apple. Open the iPhone Settings app, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, choose eSign & Invoice, and tap Cancel. You keep access until the end of the current period.

How do I restore a purchase?

In the app open Settings and tap Restore Purchases, signed in with the same Apple ID you bought with.
